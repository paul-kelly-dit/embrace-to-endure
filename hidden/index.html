Superfast WiFi Tea & Coffee (Visit the Pantries for a selection of teas and our own-blend coffee) Filtered Water (Refill during your stay with your own reusable bottle) Marshall Speaker (with smartphone connectivity) 100% organic cotton mattress (handmade in Devon) Egyptian cotton sheets & towels Natural bespoke toiletries Television (except in the Bunks) Laptop safe Hairdryer Selection of magazines & books

Each room is finished with a beautiful selection of books and magazines, real plants and feature pieces from a unique collection of original artwork.

All rooms have opening windows, a mix of timber and carpet flooring, a hanging space for clothes and reclaimed cast iron radiators to keep you warm in colder months. The utilitarian shower room is complete with Egyptian cotton towels and 100% natural toiletries.

All guests enjoy access to the Pantries for tea/coffee/water. Water at The Pilgrm, like our WiFi and the best things in life, is always free.

Telephones Minibars Desks Promotional Tent Cards Ceiling Lighting Trouser Press Air Con In Room Tea/Coffee Facilities Bathtubs

Things we don’t have at The Pilgrm: Doormen, bellboys, valet service, turndown service, room service, a business centre, vending machines, tour groups or school groups.

To us, ‘luxury’ is care. By focusing on delivering everything you truly need, and nothing you don’t, we can keep our pricing in check and our delivery honest and true.

KC Peaches

Dedicated to sourcing, roasting and serving the best coffee possible.

Workshop Coffee spend several months of each year in East Africa, South and Central America working with some of the world’s best coffee farmers, producers and cooperatives to source their coffee, which they roast in London’s Bethnal Green.

Whether taking away or taking a moment to catch your breath and enjoy the surroundings, enjoy espresso and filter coffee from their seasonal range. Postcard Teas, and baked goods from Yeast Bakery and Fortitude Bakehouse are also available alongside a considered range of coffee and brewing equipment to take home.
